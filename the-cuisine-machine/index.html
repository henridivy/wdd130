The Cuisine Machine

your tummy's favourite place

Hosting a luncheon? Preparing a meal for the whole family? Looking for a quick dish after a long day?

Look no further.

Planning a meal, day after day, can be a challenge. It isn’t always easy to figure out what to cook that will satisfy everyone’s tastes buds and won’t bore them; and this is all before checking to see if the required ingredients are available, if you have the needed tools, and if the dish is something you can prepare in your allotted timespan.

Here you will find an interesting and wide array of new dishes, to fit a variety of taste buds and diets. Different options for breakfast, lunch, and dinner, as well as sandwiches, salads, desserts, and drinks. Combine recipes to create a whole meal, suitable for the whole family or for entertaining a party. Each recipe also comes with helpful nutritional information, which includes a per serving analysis of the calorie, protein, carbohydrate, fat, sodium, and cholesterol content.

While the recipes offer a chance to try unique flavor combinations, you can adapt each one to your own taste and needs. Time management guides are provided to help make preparation easy so you are able to get everything ready to serve at the same time.

Whether you’re craving burritos, soup, or a nice stir-fried rice, we’ve got you covered!
