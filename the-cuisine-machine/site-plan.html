Overview

Purpose

Our website helps beginner and experienced homecooks discover and introduce new recipes to their everyday cooking. We do this by providing a variety of meals suitable for people with varied taste buds and diets, along with clear and helpful instructions on how to make each one. We hope our innovative ways to prepare meals inspires some interesting new flavours along the way.

Audience

Our target audience is any and all who love cooking, preparing meals, and bringing smiles to their loved-ones' faces. Our website is great for those who are keen to exploring and trying new recipes, and aren't afraid of a little experimentation. Individuals and families alike, whether new to cooking or experienced in the area, can spice up their kitchen life with our help.

Content

Home page

Hosting a luncheon? Preparing a meal for the whole family? Looking for a quick dish after a long day?

Look no further.

Planning a meal, day after day, can be a challenge. It isn’t always easy to figure out what to cook that will satisfy everyone’s tastes buds and won’t bore them; and this is all before checking to see if the required ingredients are available, if you have the needed tools, and if the dish is something you can prepare in your allotted timespan.

Here you will find an interesting and wide array of new dishes, to fit a variety of taste buds and diets. Different options for breakfast, lunch, and dinner, as well as sandwiches, salads, desserts, and drinks. Combine recipes to create a whole meal, suitable for the whole family or for entertaining a party. Each recipe also comes with helpful nutritional information, which includes a per serving analysis of the calorie, protein, carbohydrate, fat, sodium, and cholesterol content.

While the recipes offer a chance to try unique flavor combinations, you can adapt each one to your own taste and needs. Time management guides are provided to help make preparation easy so you are able to get everything ready to serve at the same time.

Whether you’re craving burritos, soup, or a nice stir-fried rice, we’ve got you covered!

Meals

Breakfast

Start your day off right with these various breakfast options. They say breakfast is the most important meal of the day. So it can be a lot of pressure to get that first meal right, as it can determine how the rest of your day will be. Here are a few breakfast options that are sure to guarantee that not only will you have a great start to your day, but also a boost of energy to get you through your daily activities!

Lunch

It’s midday and everyone’s back at the dining table. Whether you’re enjoying your lunch at home, or taking a break at work with your coworkers, lunch is supposed to restore the energy used for performing morning activities, and give you a boost to get through the rest of the day. These recipes not only taste great, but look appetizing and are low in calories. With these, lunch never has to be sad leftovers or an unfulfilling sandwich ever again.

Dinner

At last, the day is ending. You need one more kicker to make sure that no matter what enfolded during the day, you finish with a bang. Having a nutritious dinner is linked to good sleep, better choices at breakfast and lunch, and various other health benefits. For those of you eating with a party or your family, these meals can help you bond with those around you, as well as serve as a conversation starter about how good they are. Don’t shy away from the thought that preparing a healthy and delicious dinner can be a simple and enjoyable process.

Sides

While a side dish isn’t always necessary in a meal, when it is included, it enhances the dining experience. Whether its a salad, cake, or healthy drink, having a side dish compliments your main spread, turning a simple meal into a mouthwatering feast. Especially during those times you want something light and quick, these dishes will come to the rescue!
