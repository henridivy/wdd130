About Me

Henrietta Divine Gabriel

Hi, good people!

My name is Henrietta, I'm 19 (turning 20 really soon), and I'm from Nigeria but I live in Cambodia and have been living here for the past 15 years. For those of you who might not know, Cambodia is a small country in Southeast Asia, home to the Khmer language, the ancient ruins of Angkor Wat, and some incredibly nice people! I live here with my family. I'm the middle child with two siblings, my older brother (24) and my younger sister (11). My family has been members of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints since moving here. In 2020, we had the amazing opportunity to visit the Manilla Temple in Phillipines and get sealed. I currently have a calling in Primary where I get to plan activities for the children.

I've been taking online classes at BYU-I for the past two years. I'm majoring in Arts (Graphic Design) and I want to minor in Computer Programming. I've done some basic programming before in middle and highschool and I mostly enjoyed it. I love drawing, both traditionally and digitally, and would love to get into the field of graphic design, illustration, or animation as a career. I think good knowledge in computer programming would nicely aid that as so much of the creative field is moving on to the digital world everyday and I wouldn't want to fall behind. Along with taking online classes, I'm also a full-time teacher teaching lower elementary kids.

Besides drawing, some of my hobbies include eating, watching movies and TV shows, listening to music, and spending time with friends. I love playing games and sports, volleyball and football are my favourite.
